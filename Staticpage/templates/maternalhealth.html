Description

Maternal health is the health of women during pregnancy, childbirth, and the postpartum period. At this time a (would be)mother can not do the usual daily activity and the diet for the mother is also different. Based on the diet and recommended daily activity we have created a chatbot for the needs of the to be mother. She can use this chatbot to ask questions she might be having about Maternal Health and the health of the foetus.
