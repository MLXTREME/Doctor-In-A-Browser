Description

Alzheimers's disease is a progressive disease that destroys memory and other important mental functions. Brain cell connections and the cells themselves degenerate and die, eventually destroying memory and other important mental functions. The model detects 5 stages of Alzheimer's Disease.These stages are: EMCI,LMCI,MCI,AD,CN.
